Our Services

Cybercrime

I assist clients facing cybercrime charges, including system breaches, fraud, identity theft, and other online offenses. These cases often involve technical details and international elements, and I work to achieve the best possible outcome while ensuring your rights are protected in the rapidly evolving digital-legal landscape.

Extraditions

If you're facing extradition proceedings, I can guide you through the process and fight to prevent or expedite your transfer to another country. I focus on the legal, procedural, and human rights aspects of extradition cases, building strong defenses to challenge unfair or unfounded requests.

International Arrests

I assist individuals arrested abroad or wanted in different jurisdictions. Such cases require inter-state coordination, and I work alongside legal contacts in other countries to ensure you receive fair treatment and consistent legal support throughout the process.

Foreign Indictments

Receiving an indictment in a foreign country can be a stressful and anxiety-inducing experience. I help clients understand the charges against them and build a legal strategy for response – whether that involves challenging the indictment, negotiating with authorities, or planning a defense strategy.

Prosecution Agreements

In certain cases, the best outcome is achieved through negotiation. I have experience in developing agreements with prosecution authorities that lead to reduced charges, minimized penalties, or trial avoidance – while protecting your interests and long-term goals.

International Legal Network

When your case crosses borders, I engage trusted legal professionals from around the world. This network allows me to offer coordinated and well-founded defense strategies that take into account the laws and procedures of all involved countries.
